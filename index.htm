Larry E. McCalla, Ph.D.

In December 2019 I earned my Ph.D. in Learning, Design, and Technology with a focus on creativity from the University of Georgia. My dissertation is titled Activity, creativity, and design: Modeling and assessing student development in a project-based course (McCalla, 2019). My experience includes designing, implementing, and researching interventions facilitating the expansion of creative design ability. I also have expertise in instructional design and the requisite technologies.
