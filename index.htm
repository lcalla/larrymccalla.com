Larry McCalla, Ph.D.


Learning, Design, and Technology | The University of Georgia

I obtained this degree in December 2019, and my dissertation is titled Activity, creativity, and design: Modeling and assessing student development in a project-based course (McCalla, 2019). My experience includes designing, implementing, and researching interventions facilitating the expansion of creative design ability. I also have expertise in instructional design and the requisite technologies.

This site is not yet updated to reflect work done during my doctoral program, but it does show older work related to instructional design and development.
