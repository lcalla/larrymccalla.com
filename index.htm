Larry McCalla

I'm currently finalizing work for a Ph.D. at The University of Georgia in the Learning, Design, and Technology program within the College of Education's department of Career and Information Studies. My specialty is in designing, implementing, and researching situations that support the expansion of creative design ability for willing participants in a variety of formative interventions. I also have expertise in instructional design and the requisite technologies.
